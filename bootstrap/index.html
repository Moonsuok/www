  • About PRADA

    Prada was started in Milan in 1913 by Miuccia Prada, the grandfather of Miuccia Prada.

    Located inside Milan's most famous symbol, the glass-covered Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II Arcade, Prada was an exclusive store selling luxury travel items and accessories made of sophisticated technology and the best materials.

    It didn't take long before Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II's store became the favorite place to hear aristocrats and sophisticated and noble upper-class members of Europe. In 1919, Prada was appointed as the official supplier of the Italian Royal Household, and accordingly, the sentences and knot rope design of the House of Savoy were incorporated into the logo.

    The original Prada store in Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II maintains an ancient atmosphere. The Mahogany shelves, specially ordered and designed by Mario Prada to British architects, display very sophisticated leather products, shoes, and clothes from the latest collection in addition to the finest handmade bags, trunks, leather accessories, and beauty cases.

Identity

Since 1913, Prada has been synonymous with cutting-edge style. Its intellectual universe combines concept, structure and image through codes that go beyond trends. Its fashion transcends products, translating conceptuality into a universe that has become a benchmark to those who dare to challenge conventions focusing on experimentation.

1. TRANSCENDING TRENDS

Through an innovative approach, which draws inspiration from an unconventional analysis of society, Prada elaborates codes that transcend trends. Style statements born from the passionate examination of disciplines that are, at times, apparently far from fashion, such as art, film and photography, resulting into creations that reinterpret reality from unusual perspectives.

2. TRANSCENDING PRODUCTS

Unfettered from conceptual limitations and from the strict commitment to stylistic cohesion, the aesthetic codes of the brand have influenced generations of creative minds through manifestos of cultural emancipation that embrace the broadest aspects of life. Prada’s fashion transcends products bringing ideas and ideals to life through its clothes and accessories, which become tools of confident self-expression.

3. LOOKING FOR NEW PERSPECTIVES

Through her work, Miuccia Prada challenges pre-conceived aesthetic patterns. Prada is an expression of society and, as such, it changes while Prada evolves accordingly. What’s simple and classic is distorted and reconsidered in the tireless search of new perspectives. A state-of-the-art laboratory grounded on the values of instinct and cultural inclination.

4. REINTERPRETING SHARED AESTHETIC CODES

Conceptuality is Prada’s preferred form of expression in an intellectual commitment to good taste. In every collection, shared aesthetic codes are reinterpreted through an unusual perspective, while new products challenge material processes with the use of technology and know-how.

5. AN ENDLESS PLAY OF PAST, PRESENT AND FUTURE

What is considered beauty and what’s deemed questionable are deliberately mixed in an endless play of past, present and future in which bourgeois codes are often broken down and reconstructed.

Work is an important part of life in reality.

For the 2022 Autumn/Winter Prada Men's Clothes Fashion Show Stage by Miuchia Prada and Raf Simmons, AMO created a space where reality and movies (fantasy and fiction) coexist in Fonda Geone Prada. Actors served as commentators of reality to reflect the truth through their directing. Thomas Brodie-Sangster, Asa Butterfield, Jeff Goldblum, Damson Idris, Kyle MacLachlan, Tom Mercer, Jayden Michael.

PRADA suggests the dignity that has been recreated with precious leather and silk technology as a sign of practical clothing, real uniforms, and respect and value, away from the hierarchical order. It is placed side by side with narrative tailoring, a form that gives importance. Pragmatism can affect elegance, and it adds formality to these universal clothing by applying the narrative structural method to knitwear, barmour jackets, and parkas. Elegance is both a means of commemoration and a means of conveying importance.

Clothing shows the epitome of constructed modernism, a unique structure that defines shape and design. The body is emphasized, the shoulder and waist are expressed through traditional tailoring, the design is conveyed in everyday clothes, and the two styles are expressed side by side as well as integrated into one. The same solemnity is given to each genre of clothing through material and methodology, providing importance, sophistication, respect, and lasting value to all aspects of reality.

SS 2022 Menswears

TUNNEL TO JOY URGENCY OF FEELINGS UTOPIA OF NORMALITY

For the Prada 2022 Spring/Summer Men's Clothing Fashion Show at AMO, I imagined a mysterious summer vacation completed in a space where nature and artifacts interact. Walking into a red-colored winding tunnel, you will encounter a new and familiar environment. Red objects that seem stranded lie in the sand between granite rocks or float on a clear sea, and we are greeted by a surreal encounter with nature and another kind of beach structure, an uncontaminated clean landscape.

Prada 2022 Spring/Summer Men's Clothes Fashion Show was filmed in Fondagione Prada Deposito in Sardinia and Milan, Italy. Prada, as a token of gratitude to the Sardinian community, is supporting a project to restore the marine ecosystem through the restoration of the pasture of Posidonia Oceanica in the "Capo Carbonara" marine reserve with the MEDSEA Foundation. Posidonia Oceanica is an important habitat for marine life and is a Mediterranean seaweed that plays a significant role in absorbing carbon dioxide that helps mitigate the impact of climate change.

SS 2022 Womenswears

Temptation through saving

For the 2022 Spring/Summer Prada Collection, co-creative directors Miuchia Prada and Raf Simmons thought of ways to express sexuality through temptation and clothing. The process of removing clothes one by one inevitably emphasizes or exposes the body.

Existing clothes have been explored and reinterpreted. The collection follows the concept and outline of the clothes, such as the tail-long part of the back of the skirt or dress, the skeleton of the corset, and the curve of the bra, and reveals its center and most essential. Their structure is essentially connected to the body, and therefore to temptation. Traces of the past always shape our present. The skeleton or racing decoration of the stitched corset, like the shadow of the clothes, creates a new item, and rather than holding the body, the corset releases it and reveals the skin inside it.

The train used couture silk material. Each look was reconstructed by denying the traditional meaning of restraining, which made the body free. The word elegance of evening wear applies to the entire collection, and materials such as double satin and scarlet lace are used in unexpected situations. By changing the perspective, we question the meaning of these symbols and symbols. The fantasies inherent in evening wear balance with everyday reality.
